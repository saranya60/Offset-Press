About Us

Welcome to Brainwave

Brainwave is an offset printing press providing complete range of high-quality printing services. Brainwave employs the latest in the printing technology with creative designing and continuously upgrades it to be at par with the best in the world. It is located at main road kallachi in kozhikode district. With rigorous employment of quality standards at every stage of its processes, We guarantees that end product is flawless and superior to its class. We can execute printing jobs right from designing to on-target precise completion of those designs; relieving a client from first outsourcing design and then placing orders for printing. The state-of-the-art pre-press, press and post-press machineries are capable of meeting all the packaging and commercial printing requirements of wide spectrum of companies. Brainwave, with its printing and packaging facility can handle bulk orders as well as specialty works like binding of books and complex boxes. Today, Brainwave, a pioneer printing house, has several satisfied customers in our area from Pharmaceutical, Food, Cosmetic, Telecommunication, Banks and many other sectors.
